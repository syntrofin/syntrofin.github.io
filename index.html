My Journey

Technology changes. Architecture principles don't.

My career has been about guiding organizations through major technology transitions. I began in enterprise infrastructure and SAP landscapes, designing highly available systems supporting mission-critical operations.

As cloud transformed enterprise software, I moved into Salesforce architecture, leading large-scale CRM, CPQ, Billing, and integration programs.

Today, my focus is Revenue Lifecycle Management—helping organizations rethink how products, pricing, quoting, contracting, subscriptions, billing, and revenue work together as one connected business capability.

This journey allows me to connect legacy enterprise thinking with modern cloud innovation.
