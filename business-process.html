RLM Business Process

Key thing in Revenue Recognition is POB/Performance Obligation. From the Order onwards, legality kicks in. Order is a performance obligation to be fulfilled. But it is a common practice to recognize it before it is fulfilled for most companies to show to investors or stock holder if public company, th revenue coming in. Most Subscriions may not need monthly fulfillment like monthly licenses say for one year subscription. But key thing here is POB is completed only after 12 months. Revenue recognition can skip the first part shown below and directly move to next one, each month.

This does not cover accounts for payments, inventory etc which typically are outside of this subledger. Subledgers along with this ine might live in different systems and reach final GL in ERP and done reconcilation to makes sure things are correct. Systems like SAP may store copies of all sub ledgers also in (BSEG), along with GL, which gives single source of truth for accounting.
