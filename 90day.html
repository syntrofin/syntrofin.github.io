90-Day RLM Strategic Execution Plan

Principal Architect Roadmap: Transitioning to the Unified Commercial Continent
01 Days 1–30: Alignment & Revenue DNA Discovery

The first month focuses on establishing cross-functional "Handshakes" and identifying the manual "spreadsheet bridges" currently sustaining the business.

Strategic Audit

Lead deep-dive sessions with Sales, Product, Ops, and Finance. Identify "Island Silos" where data fragmentation leads to revenue leakage.

Governance Setup

Establish the Revenue Center of Excellence (RCoE). Secure executive sponsorship from the CFO and VP of Sales to protect the architectural vision.

Critical Month 1 Deliverables:

  • Strategy Selection: Formalize the choice between Greenfield, Brownfield, or Feature-First approach.
  • KPI Scorecard: Define success metrics (e.g., ARR consistency, billing dispute reduction, Quote-to-Activation velocity).
  • Process Inventory: Document all "unhappy paths" like mid-term proration and seasonal usage spikes.
02 Days 31–60: Blueprinting & Architectural Design

The second month transitions from "What" to "How," focusing on building a metadata-driven architecture that is "Correct by Design."

PCM & BRE Modeling

Enforce the shift from SKU-heavy catalogs to Attribute-Based Pricing. Design Pricing Procedures that Finance can audit easily.

Orchestration Planning

Map the Dynamic Revenue Orchestrator (DRO). Ensure hybrid deals decompose into digital and physical fulfillment paths automatically.

Critical Month 2 Deliverables:

  • The "Headless" Proof: Pass the Stage 2 Gate by proving configuration and pricing logic works via API before UI build.
  • Integration Contract: Define API endpoints for Usage Ingestion and ERP General Ledger posting.
  • Asset Lifecycle Map: Document how the system will handle Amendments and Renewals as native Asset movements.
03 Days 61–90: Build, Validate & Execute

The final month is about proving Financial Integrity. We move from technical configuration to rigorous cross-functional validation.

Persona-Based UAT

Lead testing where Sales negotiates the deal and Finance audits the resulting Revenue Recognition schedules.

Migration & Cutover

Execute reconciliation scripts to match legacy Total Contract Value (TCV) against the new Salesforce Assets.

Critical Month 3 Deliverables:

  • The "Golden Deal" Validation: Successful E2E run of a hybrid deal from Quote to Invoice to Ledger.
  • Stress Test Report: Documented success of proration logic for mid-term upgrades and cancellations.
  • RCoE Charter: A permanent governance plan for post-go-live maintenance and catalog evolution.

Day 90 Outcome: The Principal’s Handover

Pillar State at Day 90
Integrity Commercial promise matches financial realization with zero manual bridges.
Scalability Attribute-based PCM allows for new product launches without SKU bloat.
Continuity Renewals and Amendments are automated as part of the native lifecycle.
