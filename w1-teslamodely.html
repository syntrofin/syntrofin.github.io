7. Order Fulfillment & Billing (DRO)

  • Decomposition: Orders are broken down by fulfillment teams (Vehicle Manufacturing vs. Software Activation).
  • Outsourced Items: Car accessories (Roof Racks, Mats) are triggered as separate fulfillment packages.
  • Billing Policy: 100% upfront for hardware; Milestone-based or Monthly for services.
  • Usage: Aggregated monthly total for "Tesla Charging" (e.g., total miles/kWh).
