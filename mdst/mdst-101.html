Vision and Pre-Discovery (Maturity)

Before a single workflow is mapped or a sandbox is provisioned, the architectural foundation must be anchored in executive reality. This initial stage serves as the ultimate guardrail against scope creep—forcing business leaders to define the singular North Star KPI and explicitly lock the boundaries of the engagement before any technical design begins.
