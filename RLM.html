RLM Detailed Technical View

RLM Architect & Developer

Reference Table
Lifecycle Concern Lifecycle Stage Governing Concern RCA Tools
Catalog Offering Pre-quote What can be sold? Product Catalog, Offer Modeling, Attributes, Eligibility Rules, Pricing Procedures (base price determination)
Sales Intent Quote How are we willing to sell? Pricing Procedures (discounts, adjustments), Approval Policies, Document Generation
Obligation Order What must we deliver? DRO (Order Orchestration), Order Decomposition, Pricing Procedures (final pricing, commitments), Obligation Creation, Integration Procedures (ERP, Finance, Manufacturing)
Entitlement Delivery What is usable now? Milestone Management, Asset & Subscription Creation, Provisioning Integrations, Service Activation Hooks
Receivable Invoice What do we collect? Billing Schedules, Usage Rating, Invoice Generation, Tax & AR Integrations
Earned Value Revenue What is earned? Revenue Schedules, Allocation Rules, Milestone / Time / Usage Recognition, Reversals & Adjustments
