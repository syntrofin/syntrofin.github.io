RCA Data Model: Water Tanks vs. Chemical Tanks

Mapping the 16 functional steps to core Salesforce objects and Revenue Cloud engines.

Q2C Phase Functional Step Core RCA Data Model Dealer Track (Water Tanks)
High-Volume / Standardized 		Industrial Track (Chemical Tanks)
Complex / Milestone-Based
1. Quoting Configuration Product2, ProductCatalog, AttributeDefinition Product Catalog Management: Uses basic product bundles with predefined SKUs (e.g., 500-gal, 1000-gal). Attribute-Based Configuration: Uses technical attributes (e.g., acidity levels, PSI) to filter and select the correct custom liners and valves.
Pricing PricebookEntry, QuoteLineItem, ContextRule Salesforce Pricing: Executes Pricing Procedures using Lookup Tables to apply volume-tier discounts for dealers. Salesforce Pricing: Applies cost-plus markup pricing and triggers Advanced Approvals for margin protection.
Proposal QuoteDocument, ContentVersion Document Generation: Instantly generates a standard 1-page PDF order summary. OmniStudio DocGen: Pulls Quote JSON to generate a 20-page Statement of Work (SOW) including technical schematics.
CLM Contract, DocumentClause Standard Approval: Auto-approved click-wrap acceptance via community portal. Salesforce Contracts: Utilizes the Clause Library for heavy redlining of liability and indemnity terms.
2. Fulfillment Orchestration Order, OrderItem, Asset Order Generation: Converts Quote to a single standard Order for immediate fulfillment. Order Splitting: Creates multiple Orders (one for manufacturing, one for field installation labor).
Allocation Location, FulfillmentOrder Omnichannel Inventory: Reserves existing finished water tanks at the nearest regional warehouse. Procurement Trigger: Logs demand for raw materials; relies on integration to trigger purchase orders in the ERP.
Logistics Shipment, FulfillmentOrderLineItem Standard Routing: Generates basic LTL freight tracking records. Complex Logistics: Tracks oversized flatbed transport and coordinates arrival with site readiness.
Proof of Delivery ServiceAppointment, WorkOrder Status Update: Shipment status changes to "Delivered"; Order activates. Salesforce Field Service: Engineers complete the WorkOrder with a signed safety checklist; Order activates.
3. Billing Triggers BillingSchedule, InvoiceLine On-Shipment Billing: OrderItem creates a pending invoice line the moment the shipment departs. Milestone Billing: BillingSchedules dictate invoices trigger at 30% (Order), 40% (Factory Completion), 30% (Installation).
Taxation TaxTreatment, TaxRate Standard Tax Engine: Applies standard local sales tax based on the dealer's shipping address. External Tax Callout: Evaluates complex B2B tax exemptions (e.g., capital improvements for chemical plants).
Consolidation Invoice, InvoiceBatchRun Batch Schedulers: Groups all orders placed by the dealer that month onto a single, clean invoice. Project Invoicing: Generates a highly detailed, standalone invoice for that specific chemical tank project.
Accounts Rec. Payment, PaymentAllocation Salesforce Payments: Matches EDI payments or auto-drafts to the consolidated invoice. Retainage Handling: Tracks partial payments and holds final balance until the plant signs the safety certificate.
4. Rev Rec Obligations RevenueObligation Rev Rec Rules: Groups the OrderItem into a single obligation (the physical water tank). Rev Rec Rules: Splits the contract into multiple distinct obligations (Tank Hardware, Installation Labor, 5-Year Warranty).
Allocation RevenueAllocation SSP Engine: 100% of the transaction price remains allocated to the physical tank. SSP Engine: Reallocates the total discounted project price across the hardware, labor, and warranty based on their Standalone Selling Prices.
Scheduling RevenueSchedule, RevenueTransaction Point-in-Time: Generates a single transaction to recognize 100% of revenue upon shipment. Hybrid Amortization: Recognizes hardware/labor at point-in-time (acceptance), but creates a 60-month ratable schedule for the warranty.
Reporting FinanceTransaction, FinanceBook Finance Logging: Posts cleanly to the "Product Revenue" general ledger account. Finance Logging: Posts to "Project Revenue" and "Deferred Services Liability" accounts for downstream ERP consumption.
